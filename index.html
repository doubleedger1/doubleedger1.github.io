Project Space


This game was made in C++ in my penultimate year at Northumbria university on my Computer Games Programming degree. I made it during my programming for games 3 module which I scored 96% for this assignment; this taught me about pointers and object oriented programming in C++. The aim of the game is simple, fly as far as you can and accumulate the best score you possibly can before running out of fuel or dying from the environment.

Features:
  • Simple application of physics; I.E friction and gravity.
  • Simple score system based on killing and progressing.
  • Save high score system based on your previous attempts.
  • Basic sounds accompanied with menu music.
  • Difficulty system to make your environment harder.
  • Pickups to give you ammo, a shield or health.
